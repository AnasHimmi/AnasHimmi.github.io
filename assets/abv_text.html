Beeradvocate is very pleased with our results. To gain deeper insights into each cluster's characteristics, they requested additional analyses focusing on:

  • ABV Differences: Comparing the average alcohol by volume (ABV) across clusters to highlight strength variations.
  • Brewery Sizes: Identifying the typical number of beers brewed by breweries associated with each cluster.
  • Best Seasons: Determining the most popular seasons for each cluster based on review activity, helping to link beer styles to seasonal preferences.

These analyses will provide a more comprehensive understanding of the clusters, enabling more personalized recommendations and community building.
